## SEO & Content Strategy Insights

### Pillar Content Structure

**Procedure Hubs:**

- Comprehensive guides (3,000+ words)

- Before/after galleries
- Cost breakdowns
- Recovery timelines
- Risk/complication transparency

**Supporting Content:**

- "Is X worth it?" comparison articles
- Celebrity procedure analysis

### Technical SEO Requirements

- Core Web Vitals optimization (critical for medical)
- Mobile-first design (60%+ mobile traffic)
- Schema markup (MedicalProcedure, FAQPage)
- E-E-A-T signals (expertise, authoritativeness, trustworthiness)
- Author bios with credentials

### Local SEO Consideration

- City/region landing pages
- "Best plastic surgeon in [city]" targeting
- Google Business Profile optimization
